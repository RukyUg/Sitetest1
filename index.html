What we make

Immersive formats

We design and produce across the full spectrum of spatial media — from intimate VR pieces to monumental fulldome screenings.

Virtual Reality

Cinematic and interactive VR experiences for headsets and immersive installations.

Fulldome

360° dome productions for planetariums, festival domes, and ceremony spaces.

360° Video

Documentary and narrative 360° films for impact, education, and festivals.

Augmented Reality

Geolocated and marker-based AR layers that extend physical spaces into living stories.

Virtual Tours

Interactive spatial tours for cultural heritage sites, museums, and natural landscapes.

Creative Direction

Afrofuturist visual identity and spatial narrative consulting for cultural institutions.

Forest of the Forgotten Codes

An Afrofuturist fulldome experience and collective ceremony game exploring indigenous futures across the continent's dome network.

About the studio

We keep
what matters

Keepers XR is an immersive storytelling and creative studio founded in Kampala, Uganda. We believe spatial media can democratize how cultures document themselves, inspire climate action, and imagine futures on their own terms.
